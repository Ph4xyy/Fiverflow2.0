# Custom Templates

Design and manage custom invoice templates that match your brand and streamline your invoicing workflow.

## Overview

Custom templates in FiverFlow allow you to create professional, reusable invoice designs with your branding, preferred layout, and standard terms. This saves time and ensures consistency across all your invoices.

## Creating a Template

1. **Go to Templates**: Navigate to Invoices â†’ Templates
2. **Click "New Template"**: Start with a blank template or modify an existing one
3. **Design Your Layout**: Customize colors, fonts, and sections
4. **Add Your Branding**: Upload your logo and set brand colors
5. **Configure Sections**: Include or exclude sections like terms, notes, or footer
6. **Set Default Values**: Define default line items, payment terms, or notes
7. **Save Template**: Name your template and save it for future use

## Template Design Elements

### Header Section
- **Company Logo**: Upload your business logo (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
- **Company Name**: Your business name and tagline
- **Contact Information**: Address, phone, email, website
- **Custom Graphics**: Add visual elements or patterns

### Invoice Details
- **Invoice Number Format**: Choose numbering style
- **Date Format**: Set preferred date display
- **Currency**: Default currency for the template
- **Language**: Template language and translations

### Body Section
- **Line Item Layout**: Table or list format
- **Column Options**: Description, quantity, rate, total, tax
- **Additional Fields**: Custom columns for SKUs, categories, etc.
- **Subtotals**: Tax breakdown, discounts, fees

### Footer Section
- **Terms and Conditions**: Payment terms, late fees, return policy
- **Thank You Message**: Custom closing message
- **Contact Support**: Support contact information
- **Legal Notices**: Tax IDs, registration numbers

## Branding Options

### Colors
- **Primary Color**: Main brand color for headers and accents
- **Secondary Color**: Secondary brand elements
- **Text Colors**: Customizable text colors for readability
- **Background**: Optional background color or watermark

### Typography
- **Font Family**: Choose from web-safe or custom fonts
- **Font Sizes**: Adjust sizes for different elements
- **Font Weights**: Bold, regular, or light variations
- **Line Height**: Optimize readability

### Layout
- **Template Width**: Full width or centered content
- **Margins and Padding**: Adjust spacing
- **Section Order**: Reorder invoice sections
- **Alignment**: Left, center, or right alignment

## Managing Templates

### Edit Templates
- Update design elements anytime
- Modify default values
- Change branding or layout
- Test template with sample data

### Duplicate Templates
- Create variations of existing templates
- Copy templates for different services
- A/B test different designs

### Delete Templates
- Remove unused templates
- Archive old templates instead of deleting
- Recover deleted templates within 30 days

## Best Practices

- **Consistency**: Use the same template across similar services
- **Professional Design**: Keep designs clean and professional
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Ensure templates look good on all devices
- **Test Printing**: Verify templates print correctly
- **Regular Updates**: Refresh branding as your business evolves

## Tips

- Create separate templates for different service types
- Use a simple template for quick invoices
- Save detailed templates for complex projects
- Include legal requirements specific to your industry
- Test templates with real data before using
